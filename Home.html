Our technology

The concept of cell educating was born fifteen years ago and matured throughout the academic research career of the founders. After the creation of PredictCan Biotechnologies, proof-of-concepts were performed to validate assumptions and to demonstrate that this technology can be successful in precision medicine.

Our patented cell educating technology (PCT/EP2024/052109) exploits the intrinsic plasticity of cell lines by inducing subject-dependent phenotypic changes to better model healthy or pathological conditions.
